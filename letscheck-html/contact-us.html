Getting to CTIC:

  • If you are driving from the city:
    • Drive along the AYER RAJAH EXPRESSWAY (AYE) towards Jurong
    • Exit AYE (exit 8) and head towards SOUTH BUONA VISTA ROAD
    • After passing the National University Health System building on your right, turn RIGHT into KENT RIDGE ROAD at the traffic junction
    • Keep LEFT and turn LEFT into PRINCE GEORGE’S PARK road
    • Continue on PRINCE GEORGE’S PARK road (down slope)
    • The Innovation 4.0 Building is the LAST building at the end of slope on your RIGHT (right after the Temesak Life Sciences Lab)
    • Park in any of the white carpark lots on the left of the building (Carpark 11B)
    • Head to the main lobby and take the lift up to the 4th level
  • If you are driving from Pasir Panjang Road:
    • From PASIR PANJANG ROAD, turn into NUS ENTRANCE C and into HENG MUI KENG TERRACE
    • Keep RIGHT at the fork in front of the NUS Business School
    • Immediately take the first LEFT towards PRINCE GEORGE’S PARK/RESEARCH LINK
    • After passing under the NUS Business School Biz 2 Building and its open-air carpark (Carpark 11), turn left into Innovation 4.0 which the first building up the slope
    • Park in any of the white carpark lots on the left of the building (Carpark 11B)
    • Head to the main lobby and take the lift up to the 4th level
  • If you are taking public transport:
    • Take MRT (Circle Line) to the Kent Ridge Station (CC24)
    • Take Exit A and walk through the underground tunnel to the bus stop OPPOSITE the station
    • Take the free NUS shuttle bus service A2
    • Alight at the bus-stop at TCOMS, 2 stops later
    • Cross the road and walk down the slope - the innovation 4.0 is the WHITE building past the Temasek Life Sciences Laboratory
    • Head to the main lobby and take the lift up to the 4th level
    Turn left from the lift and go straight to the end of the corridor where Centre for Internet and Community is located.
